My Dream Vacation: Cancun

I would love to go to Cancun. Here are some fun things I'd love to do at the resort and other things I've learned!

Sit by the Beach

My favorite activity on vacation is sitting by a body of water and drinking cocktails. Bonus points if the cocktails are all-you-can-drink!

Swim in the Pool

If I can't sit by the beach, the pool is the second best thing! I love using a big floaty and also drinking cocktails.

Ziplining

After ziplining once in college, I really want to go again! I think it'd be so fun to zipline over a river or waterfall.

Why Cancun?

I've decided on Cancun because of the awesome things I've heard about it. My friends that have went absolutely loved it.

"Two days into the trip, we rebooked for next year. It's amazing!"

Kat and Ryan

There is a downside, to this trip, however. It is much more expensive than if you were to vacation somewhere in the United States. Resort costs, plus the airfare, can run you thousands of dollars.

In total, the cost is around $6,000 USD for two people. Save wisely!
